Fairy Tale (Fairy Tale of Kings), 1909 — M.K. Čiurlionis
This painting was created to honor Lithuania’s fairytale heritage at a time when the nation’s identity was under threat from Russian aggression. Fairytales are at the heart of Lithuanian culture, so the two figures exchanging a glowing mystical world is a symbolization of sharing stories and imagination, an act of cultural resistance.
